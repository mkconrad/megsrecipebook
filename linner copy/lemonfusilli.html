lemon fusilli with arugula

serves: 4-5

meg's rating:

who doesn't like lemon & arugula #lovely

ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic, 2 cloves
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 3 lemons
  • salt & pepper, to taste
  • 1 lb dried fusilli pasta
  • 1/2 lb baby arugula
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved

directions!

  1. heat olive oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat
  2. add garlic & cook for 1 min, then add cream, zest & juice of 2 lemons, 2 tsp salt, & 1 tsp pepper
  3. bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer for 15-20 mins, until it starts to thicken
  4. bring large pot of water to a boil, add 1 tbsp salt & pasta
  5. cook al dente (according to directions on the package) around 12 mins, stirring occasionally
  6. drain pasta and return to pot
  7. immediately add the cream mixture & cook over medium-low heat for 3 min, until the pasta has absorbed most of the sauce
  8. pour the hot pasta into a large bowl & add arugula, parmesan, & tomatoes
  9. cut the last lemon in half, (lengthwise), slice it 1/4 in thick and add slices to pasta
  10. toss well, season to taste, and serve hot

enjoy!
