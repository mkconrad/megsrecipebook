cream of pesto pasta w asparagus & corn

serves: 2

meg's rating:

listen to: louis prima, zuma & drink some nice burgundy wine #drank

ingredients:

  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 ear of corn
  • 10 spears of asparagus, discard bottom quarter & cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 fresh/dried hot pepper, diced or crushed
  • 8 oz angel hair egg pasta
  • 1/3 cup half & half cream
  • 4 tbsp (3 oz) pesto sauce
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

directions!

  1. boil water and cook pasta according to directions
  2. in saute pan on med-high heat, melt 2 tbsp butter
  3. saute asparagus, corn, garlic, & hot pepper for 5-8 mins until corn begins to brown. reserve in a warmed bowl
  4. in same pan, over low heat, melt remaining 2 tbsp butter and slowly stir in cream, parmesan, pesto, and pepper. stir and allow to simmer over lowest heat for 2 mins
  5. drain pasta and add to pesto cream sauce. toss.
  6. serve on warmed plates/bowls. top w asparagus and corn #nice

enjoy!
